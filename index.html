A Clever Riddle

Can you solve this tricky riddle?

"I can be deep but also just on the surface. Sometimes you see me, sometimes no one notices me. Only time makes sure I perish. What am I?"
Answer: A Wound

While many people guess a shadow, a wound is the most logical answer. Wounds can be deep cuts or surface scratches. They can be exposed or hidden from view. Finally, time heals all wounds, causing them to perish.
